Product description¶

By taking advantage of the complementarity between camera and motion sensor, MYNT® EYE standard version provides the develpment hardware which has higher precision, lower cost, easy way of deployment and the ability to recognize faces and objects.So far, [Binocular + IMU] is still at the leading edge of SLAM development.

As a hardware product for in-depth research and development of stereo vision computing applications, MYNT® EYE standard version can be widely used in the field of Visual positioning navigation (vSLAM), including Visual real-time positioning navigation system of Driverless Vehicle and Robot, Visual positioning system of UAV, Obstacle avoidance navigation system for Driverless Vehicle, Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), etc. At the same time, it can be used in field of Visual recognition, including Stereoscopic face recognition, Three-dimensional object recognition, space motion tracking, 3D gestures and somatosensory recognition. And of course, you can use it for measurement which includes Assisted driving system (ADAS), Binocular volume calculation, Industrial visual screening, etc.

With self-development camera techniques, such as frame synchronization, automatic exposure, and white balance control, etc. MYNT® EYE standard version can output high-precision synchronized image source, and help reduce the difficulty of algorithms development。At the same time, it is equipped with six axis sensor (IMU) and Infrared active light detector (IR). Six axis sensor (IMU) can provides complementary and correction for the research of visual positioning algorithms, also be applicable for the research of Visual inertial mileage algorithms (VIO), it helps improve positioning precision. Infrared active light detector (IR) can help to resolve the problem that it is difficult to recognize the indoor white wall and no-textured objects, and improve the precision of image source recognition,

To guarantee the data quality of the camera product, we have done calibration for binocular before they left the factory. Also, the product have passed lots of hardware stability tests, such as high temperature and humidity continuous work, high temperature and humidity continuous operation, low temperature dynamic aging, high temperature work, low temperature storage, cold and heat shock of the whole machine, sinusoidal vibration and random vibration, to ensure the stability and reliability of the quality. It not only can be used for the development of product and techniques, but also can be used in product mass production which will shorten the time from development to production.

MYNT® EYE has been recognized by many well-known companies at home and abroad since it was introduced. In addition to the standard version of the [S] series that has already been released, the [D] product with built-in deep computing power will also be launched in the future. Clients can choose suitable products based on the computing power and computing architecture of the target platform.

To become the world’s top Stereo Vision Computing Technology solution provider, We have been constantly optimizing and improving our hardware and software to make MYNT® EYE be the best platform for stereo vision technology development and application.
